# Competitive Analysis

Compare products, services, or companies side-by-side.

## Description

This skill helps analyze competitors by gathering information and creating comparison matrices, strength/weakness assessments, and strategic recommendations.

## Instructions

When performing competitive analysis:
1. Research each competitor's features, pricing, strengths, weaknesses
2. Create structured comparison tables
3. Identify gaps and opportunities
4. Generate actionable insights

For comparison matrices:
- List competitors in columns
- Use consistent evaluation criteria (features, pricing, support)
- Include ratings or scores where applicable

For strategic analysis:
- Highlight unique selling propositions
- Note areas of competitive advantage
- Identify market opportunities

## Examples

- "Compare Slack vs Teams vs Discord for team communication"
- "Create a competitive analysis of CRM tools"
- "What are the pros and cons of Product A vs Product B?"

## Parameters

- competitors: List of competitors to compare
- criteria: Comparison criteria (features, pricing, reviews)
- format: Output format (table, document, spreadsheet)
- includePricing: Include pricing information (default: true)
