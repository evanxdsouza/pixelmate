# Email

Compose and prepare email content.

## Description

This skill helps draft professional email messages.

## Instructions

When composing emails:
1. Start with a clear subject line
2. Use appropriate greeting
3. Be concise and professional
4. Include clear call to action

## Examples

- "Write a follow-up email to a client"
- "Draft a meeting request email"
- "Create a professional response to the inquiry"

## Parameters

- to: Recipient email or name (required)
- subject: Email subject line (required)
- body: Email body content (required)
- tone: Tone of the email (optional, default: professional)
